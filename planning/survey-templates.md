# Survey Templates Library

Pre-built survey templates for common use cases. Users can start from any template and customize as needed.

---

## 1. Customer Satisfaction (CSAT)

**Use Case:** Measure customer satisfaction with products, services, or support interactions.

**Questions:**
1. Overall, how satisfied are you with [Company/Product]? *(Rating: 1-5 stars)*
2. How well did we meet your expectations? *(Rating: 1-5)*
3. How likely are you to continue using our product/service? *(Rating: 1-5)*
4. What did we do well? *(Open text)*
5. What could we improve? *(Open text)*
6. How would you rate the quality of our customer service? *(Rating: 1-5)*
7. Would you recommend us to a friend or colleague? *(NPS: 0-10)*

---

## 2. Employee Engagement

**Use Case:** Assess employee satisfaction, motivation, and workplace culture.

**Questions:**
1. How satisfied are you with your current role? *(Rating: 1-5)*
2. I feel valued for my contributions at work. *(Likert: Strongly Disagree → Strongly Agree)*
3. I have the resources I need to do my job effectively. *(Likert)*
4. My manager provides clear direction and support. *(Likert)*
5. I see opportunities for growth and advancement here. *(Likert)*
6. I would recommend this company as a great place to work. *(NPS: 0-10)*
7. What do you enjoy most about working here? *(Open text)*
8. What would make this a better place to work? *(Open text)*
9. On a scale of 1-10, how likely are you to still be working here in one year? *(Rating: 1-10)*

## 3. Market Research (Product Testing)

**Use Case:** Gather feedback on new products, features, or concepts before launch.

**Questions:**
1. How appealing is this product concept to you? *(Rating: 1-5)*
2. How likely would you be to purchase this product? *(Rating: Very Unlikely → Very Likely)*
3. What do you like most about this product? *(Open text)*
4. What concerns do you have about this product? *(Open text)*
5. How does this compare to similar products you've used? *(Multiple Choice: Much Worse, Worse, About the Same, Better, Much Better)*
6. What features are most important to you? *(Ranking or Multi-select)*
7. What price would you expect to pay for this product? *(Multiple Choice: price ranges)*
8. Who would you buy this product for? *(Multiple Choice: Myself, Family, Gift, Business)*
9. Any additional comments or suggestions? *(Open text)*

## Template Metadata

Each template includes:
- **Name**: Template title
- **Description**: Brief explanation of use case
- **Category**: Customer, Employee, Event, Forms, Research
- **Estimated Time**: Completion time (e.g., "2-3 minutes")
- **Question Count**: Number of questions
- **AI-Optimized**: Whether questions are designed for AI analysis

## Usage Notes
- Templates can be fully customized after selection
- Questions can be reordered, edited, or deleted
- AI will suggest additional questions based on the template
- Branching logic can be added to any template
